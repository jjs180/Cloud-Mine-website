SOUTH CITY

The core aspect of this project was to refresh the visual brand of an existing shopping mall that hadn’t been updated in over a decade. This include a new logo design and color scheme throughout. The logo elements together form the ‘S’, with the upper element indicating the ‘C’, but they also represent day and night, indicating their long opening hours.
