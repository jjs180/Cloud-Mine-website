THE BIGGER PICTURE

Cloud Mine Creative is very much embedded in the culture of Cloud Mine Consulting. We are business process engineers, marketing strategists, developers, and designers working in unison towards a common goal: the unification of the creative aspects of marketing and lead acquisition and the technical aspects of streamlined business processes.

We take a holistic view of the business process and our creative studio is at the vanguard. By unifying creative, marketing, and business process Cloud Mine can seamlessly integrate all your lead acquisition channels with your CRM systems. By doing so, we can attribute leads to specific campaigns and sources, track the entire customer journey and drill down on your return on investment from your marketing.

Cloud Mine Creative is not just a creative supplement to the core Cloud Mine Consulting service, but a full creative tour de force in its own right. We have built a top notch design team that reaches the highest standards in design, inspiration and creativity. Our creative studio follows the mold of our overarching organizational structure to cut out the fluff of a traditional creative agency and get right down to core creative services, delivering results that exceed expectations at prices that small-medium sized business can afford.
