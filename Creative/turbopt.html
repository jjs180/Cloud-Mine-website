TURBO PT

CloudMine was engaged as Turbo PT's software marketing agency to architect a full brand reboot starting with a new logo and traditional print-based campaigns in trade publications. The goal was to change the market perception of Turbo PT from a traditional server-based software provider to modern and intuitive cloud-based software.

CloudMine proceeded to develop a new mobile responsive website with strong calls to action and a well-structured content funnel. CloudMine managed an SEO campaign that resulted in a 10x increase in organic search traffic and improved search engine ranking positions for high-value and industry-specific keyword phrases. CloudMine designs Turbo PT email marketing campaigns and Turbo PT utilizes CloudMine's advertising platform for Search Engine Marketing which has enabled Turbo PT to lower its cost per click, increase visitors, and track and convert more leads. Cloud Mine has also designed Turbo PT’s convention booth for the APTA’s Combined Sections Meeting and Private Practice Section. As Turbo PT’s creative and marketing agency, we delivered a unified brand concept spanning virtually every conceivable medium and marketing channel.
